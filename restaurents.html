FAMOUS

RESTAURENTS



Restaurants in Maldives offer unique dining experiences, but Heritance Aarah takes this to a whole new level as the resort is home to an impressive array of dining options, from exclusive pod dining at Ginifati to the world-class buffet at Ranba Restaurant.


The overwater settings of Ambula, Ralu, and Hathaa restaurants enhance the sensory experience, presenting cuisines from Sri Lanka, the Mediterranean, and Asia. Casual dining is at its best at the Pizza Shack or by the pool at Bandhi Bar. Overwater bars like the Sky Bar and Falhu provide exclusive cocktails, fine spirits, and stunning views.

GINIFATI PRIVATE DINING

With views of endless rippling waves, Ginifati private dining in Maldives means an opportunity to experience a private and exclusive pod setting.

Enjoy a fine selection of the day’s catch, prepared to your liking, and paired with a selection of fine wines or spirits along with the restaurant’s signature rum punch cocktail.

RANBA RESTAURANT

For sophisticated travellers seeking the best restaurant in Maldives, Ranba Restaurant is a buffet Restaurant offering the world on a plate and a reflection of real, everyday international cuisine.

Enjoy an impressive array; from east Asian hawker stalls to a western farmers’ market

Ambula Overwater Restaurant

Ambula Overwater Restaurant presents an exceptional dining experience that showcases the best of Maldivian and Sri Lankan cuisine.

This restaurant serves contemporary Heritance cuisine, with an inventive, eight-course tasting menu experience which encompasses exotic dishes from both islands.

Ralu Overwater Restaurant

Discover one of the top restaurants in Maldives. Inspired by the French and Italian Mediterranean brasseries lining the Riviera coast, Ralu Overwater Restaurant overlooks the Indian Ocean and is adjacent to the Ralu Overwater Lounge Bar.

The restaurant offers an all-day à la carte menu with live action stations where you can see our chefs create popular classics. A wine sommelier is on call to recommend the best pairings with your dishes.
