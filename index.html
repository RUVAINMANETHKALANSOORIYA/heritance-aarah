Our Rooms

At Heritance Aarah, our collection of villas and suites is designed to seamlessly blend indulgent and contemporary comforts with traditional aesthetics. Rise to the gentle rhythm of the waves each day and enjoy a slower pace, as you dive into the timeless magic of life at our luxury resort. Maldives offers a range of luxury accommodation and the experience you get at Heritance Aarah is unique and special. Endemic vegetation grown on our island between each of our land villas, acts as a natural boundary wall for utmost privacy. A unique feature of our accommodation is that every villa is stocked with in-villa snorkeling gear, allowing you to experience stunning coral and marine life, any time of the day.
